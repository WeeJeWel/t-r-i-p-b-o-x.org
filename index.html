TRIPBOX is a project to connect psychedelic explorers and share their wisdom.

This is how it works
You donate ten euro for shipping and our effort.
You wait patiently until it's your turn.

The tripbox is sent to you. You trip.
You open the box. You enjoy the artifacts inside.

You keep one thing as a memory. You put something new in.
You share your story in the journal.

We e-mail you a shipping label.
You ship the box to your fellow tripper.
